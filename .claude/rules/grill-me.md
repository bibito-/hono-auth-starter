Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

Keep each question to one or two sentences. Do not pile on preamble, traces, or restated explanations — over-explaining blurs intent and wastes time by creating a false sense of understanding. Only ask about points that are genuinely core and not yet agreed; never ask a question merely to make the user restate something already settled or that you already know the answer to. 詳細: [[feedback-grill-concise]]

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
